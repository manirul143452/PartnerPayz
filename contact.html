Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about PartnerPayz.

Is PartnerPayz secure for managing our finances?

Yes, PartnerPayz uses bank-level security protocols including 256-bit encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. We never store your banking passwords, and all data is protected with the highest security standards.

How do I add my partner to my account?

Once you've created your account, navigate to Settings > Partner Integration and enter your partner's email address. They'll receive an invitation to join your financial partnership, which they can accept to link your accounts.

What's the difference between free and premium plans?

The free plan includes basic financial tracking, budget creation, and expense splitting features. Premium plans add advanced analytics, financial advisor access, tax planning tools, and unlimited goal tracking for couples who want more comprehensive financial management.

Can we connect our existing bank accounts?

Yes, PartnerPayz integrates with over 10,000 financial institutions worldwide. You can securely connect your bank accounts, credit cards, loans, and investment accounts to get a complete picture of your financial situation as a couple.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to Settings > Billing > Subscription and clicking "Cancel Subscription." Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
